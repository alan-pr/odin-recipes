Lasagna

Description

A scrumptious lasagna is a multi-layered masterpiece that embodies the heart and soul of traditional Italian cuisine. Each layer consists of silky, tender pasta sheets that have been cooked to perfection, holding between them a rich and hearty ragù. This meat sauce, often a blend of ground beef and pork, is simmered slowly with tomatoes, garlic, onions, and a medley of herbs, infusing it with deep, robust flavors. Interspersed with the ragù are luxurious layers of creamy, smooth béchamel sauce and generous sprinklings of grated Parmesan cheese, adding a velvety texture and a nutty, savory taste that perfectly balances the richness of the meat. Completing this culinary delight is a top layer of melted mozzarella cheese that bubbles and browns to a golden hue, creating a visually tempting crust that's irresistible. Each forkful of lasagna is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, from the soft pasta to the savory sauces and gooey cheese, making it a comforting and satisfying dish that's beloved by many.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Cloves Garlic
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Precook noodles
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Add sauce
  4. Mix Ricotta layer
  5. Assemble the lasagna
  6. Bake
  7. Cool and serve!
