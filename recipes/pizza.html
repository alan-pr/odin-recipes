Pizza

Description

Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at a high temperature
  2. Prepare dough
  3. Add sauce
  4. Add mozzarella cheese
  5. Add pepperoni
  6. Brush the crust
  7. Bake the pizza
  8. Serve and enjoy!
